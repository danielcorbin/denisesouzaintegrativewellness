Thermography

A whole-body window into inflammation, circulation, and dysfunction, years before it becomes symptomatic.

What it is

A full-body picture, not a single-area snapshot.

Thermography uses infrared imaging to map heat and circulation patterns across your entire body, not just one region. It identifies areas of inflammation, blockage, stagnation, and imbalance across multiple systems: lymphatic, digestive, hormonal, and nervous.

Where conventional structural tests look for things like tumors, thermography looks for functional dysfunction, often detectable 8 to 10 years before it would otherwise be visible or felt.

The goal isn't to look at one part of you in isolation. It's to see the whole picture, so we can catch dysfunction early and work with your body before symptoms ever show up.

Why thermography

Safe. Simple. Whole-body.

No radiation

100% safe, with no contraindications. Can be repeated as often as needed.

No contact, no pain

Non-invasive and compression-free. Nothing touches your body during the scan.

FDA recognized adjunct

Used alongside, not in place of, other diagnostic tools and your physician's care.

Earliest detection

Functional changes can be identified years before structural tests would catch them.

What it covers

Multiple systems, one scan.

A full-body thermography scan can reveal patterns across your lymphatic, digestive, hormonal, and nervous systems, giving you and Denise a much fuller picture of your overall health than a single targeted test ever could. This includes breast tissue, but breast health is just one piece of a much larger picture, not the focus of the scan.

It's a proactive tool: as useful for confirming that your current lifestyle is working as it is for catching something early.
