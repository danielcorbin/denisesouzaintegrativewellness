## A note on style
Across all of these: natural light over flash, candid/real over posed, and
nothing needs professional photography. Phone photos are completely fine as
long as they're reasonably sharp and well-lit. Wide shots are more useful than
tight crops since we can always crop in later, but we can't zoom out from a
photo that's already too tight.

If you have multiple options for any of these, send them all. More to choose
from is always better than not enough.
