AO Scan

A non-invasive technology that reads your body's frequencies and identifies where balance is needed.

What it is

Every cell in your body has a frequency. The AO Scan listens.

The AO Scan is a non-invasive, frequency-based wellness tool developed by Solex. Using subtle bio-frequencies and electromagnetic signals, it communicates with the body to identify areas where energetic balance may be supported, and generates detailed feedback reports to inform personalized wellness decisions.

It draws on a library of over 120,000 Blueprint Frequencies representing the body's cells, tissues, organs, and emotional states, comparing your personal frequency patterns against these blueprints to highlight areas that may benefit from support.

"If you want to find the secrets of the universe, think in terms of energy, frequency, and vibration." — Nikola Tesla

How it works

A whole-body picture in minutes.

The scan is conducted using a handheld device and a specialized bone-conduction headset. The technology sends gentle electromagnetic signals through the body and reads the returning frequencies, comparing them to optimal patterns to identify imbalances across systems, organs, and tissues.

Results are delivered in a detailed report you review together with Denise, who uses the findings to inform your personalized wellness plan. The AO Scan also includes Inner Voice technology, which analyzes vocal tones to identify emotional imbalances and generates personalized audio frequencies to support emotional awareness and balance.
