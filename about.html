About Denise

A guide, not just a practitioner.

Twenty years of holistic practice, built on her own journey back to health.

My story

Wellness found me before I found it.

My own path into holistic health started in the late 1980s with a Gilbert Syndrome diagnosis, followed by my children's food sensitivities. Conventional answers fell short, so I went looking for better ones. That search became a career.

Today I hold a Master's with High Honors in Holistic Nutrition and a Bachelor's in Psychology, and I'm a Certified Thermography Specialist, LEAP Trained Therapist, and Quantum Living Associate. But the credentials are tools, not the point. The point is helping you understand what your body is telling you.

Outside the practice

Raleigh has been home since 2006.

My husband Jerry and I moved here from Massachusetts, drawn by the outdoors and the long summer days. That same love of nature shapes how I think about healing: nothing forced, nothing rushed, just the right conditions for the body to do what it already knows how to do.

When I'm working with a client, my goal is simple: teach you enough about your own body that you leave more confident, not more dependent.
