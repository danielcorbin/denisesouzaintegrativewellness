Privacy Policy

How we handle information on this website.

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

This website is informational. It does not collect personal data through forms, accounts, or online bookings, and it does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking tools of any kind.

Information you provide directly

If you contact us by phone or email, the information you choose to share, such as your name, phone number, email address, or details about your health and wellness goals, is used only to respond to your inquiry and to provide care if you become a client. We do not sell, rent, or share this information with third parties for marketing purposes.

New client intake forms

This site links to a downloadable patient intake form. Completing and submitting this form is done outside of this website (in person or by the method Denise specifies), and any health information you share through that process is handled as part of your care, consistent with standard practice for health and wellness providers.

Cookies and tracking

This site does not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes and does not run analytics software. Our website host (Vercel) may collect basic, anonymized technical logs (such as server requests) as part of standard infrastructure operation; this data is not used by us to identify or track individual visitors.

Third-party links

This site may link to external resources, such as the downloadable intake form or directions via Google Maps. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third-party sites once you leave denisesouzaintegrativewellness.com.

Children's privacy

This website is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes to the website or how it operates. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent update.
