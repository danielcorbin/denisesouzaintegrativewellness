Signature program

Rooted Renewal

A progressive program designed to heal and rewire emotions, the nervous system, and the brain.

How it works

Four levels. Each one complete on its own.

Rooted Renewal is a structured, layered process that works with emotional patterns, the nervous system, and the brain's natural capacity to rewire, addressing root causes rather than managing symptoms.

Each level builds intentionally on the one before it, moving from awareness to repatterning to deep integration, yet every layer is complete and beneficial on its own. You can enter where you are and still experience meaningful change.

As emotional patterns are processed and neural pathways reorganize, the body is relieved of carrying unresolved stress, creating the conditions for lasting balance and restoration.

Level One

Leaves

Emotional Awareness & Insight — You cannot change what you cannot see.

Leaves is the entry point into Rooted Renewal. This level focuses on emotional awareness through vocal frequency analysis, helping clients understand what their body and nervous system may be communicating beneath conscious awareness. Even when an emotional experience is no longer consciously remembered, its imprint can still influence stress responses, behaviors, and physical symptoms. Leaves brings those patterns into awareness gently, without overwhelm.

Timeframe

One month

  • One month of Inner Voice platform access for recordings, analysis, and personalized audio support

  • Lifestyle guidance supporting the physical expressions of unresolved emotions
  • Ongoing email support between sessions

Best for those who

  • Want a gentle, non-invasive entry point

Branches

Branches builds on emotional awareness by addressing the beliefs and thought patterns shaped by emotional experience. Once patterns are identified, this level supports intentional mental and emotional repatterning through repetition, resonance, and conscious participation, reinforcing true beliefs and breaking through emotional blocks.

Level Four · Most Advanced

Soil

Foundational Emotional & Belief Transformation — Change where patterns are formed.

Together, these work at the neural and physiological level, helping the nervous system move out of long-standing stress habits and reinforcing adaptive patterns for the long term.
