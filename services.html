Food Sensitivity Testing

Many chronic symptoms, including fatigue, joint pain, digestive trouble, and skin conditions, trace back to foods your body is quietly reacting to. Denise uses the LEAP protocol and MRT (Mediator Release Test), the most accurate and comprehensive blood test available for identifying food and food chemical reactions.

MRT checks your immune system's response to 170 foods and chemicals, measuring how white blood cells react to identify hidden inflammation-producing triggers. The results are used to create an individualized ImmunoCalm nutrition plan that significantly reduces or eliminates symptoms, resulting in a healthier lifestyle and well-balanced nutrition. Whether reactions are dose-dependent, delayed, or driven by the innate or adaptive immune system, MRT provides the most complete picture available.

Blood Cell Analysis

Blood Cell Analysis reveals the biological makeup of each individual, with different cellular patterns commonly linked to specific health challenges. A single drop of blood, drawn by a simple fingertip prick, is examined under high magnification and viewed together in real time on a screen, revealing what's happening inside your body right now.

By seeing what is happening within the cells throughout the body, abnormalities can be addressed using an all-natural approach. The goal is to discover the origin of imbalance and identify which cellular patterns could be contributing to current or future challenges, addressing the root rather than the individual symptoms.
